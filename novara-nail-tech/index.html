About the Studio

More than a
manicure. It's
self-care.

Hi, I'm Lola — a nail artist based in Liverpool's Baltic Triangle. I've spent five years refining my craft, building a small and loyal clientele who come to me for precision, creativity, and a genuinely relaxing experience.

My studio is calm, curated, and just for you. No rushing, no walk-ins. Every appointment is dedicated time for you to breathe, catch up on your podcast, and leave with nails you're genuinely obsessed with.

Lola x
