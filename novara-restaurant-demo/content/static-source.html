Our Story

Born in
Kingston.
Cooked in
Liverpool.

Calabash was born from a kitchen table in Kingston, Jamaica — passed down through three generations of cooks who believed food was the highest expression of love and culture.

Chef Marcus Williams brought those recipes to Liverpool in 2009. Since then, Calabash has become a fixture in the Baltic Triangle — a place where the Caribbean diaspora eats, celebrates, and feels at home, and where curious Liverpudlians discover flavours they'll come back for again and again.

Marcus Williams
Head Chef & Founder
