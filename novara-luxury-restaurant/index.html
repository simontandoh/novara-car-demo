Our Philosophy

Precision.
Restraint.
Memory.

At Onyx, we believe that the most memorable meals are built on clarity — a single ingredient at its peak, a technique applied with total confidence, a pause on the palate that stays with you long after you've left the table. Seven courses. No performance. Just food that earns its place on the plate.

The Chef

Trained in
Lyon. Built
in London.

Elian Markov trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens across France and Spain before opening Onyx in 2017. His approach is rooted in classical French technique, applied with a restraint that lets British ingredients speak without interruption.

He does not believe in spectacle. He believes in the plate.
